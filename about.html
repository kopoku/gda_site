ABOUT

The Graphic Design Awards is organised by White Walnut Ghana Limited to recognise graphic design talents in Ghana supported by Communication Design Department of the Faculty of Art, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology and the Ghana Institution of Graphic Artists.

Vision

Graphic Design Awards Ghana is an award scheme developed to recognise and celebrate talented Graphic Designers and also to motivate and inspire others. It is also to showcase to the business community the best of graphic design in Ghana. It is envisaged to serve as a platform for the graphic design companies to interact and network. It will be an annual event.

Mission

Graphic Design Awards Ghana is to:

  • Celebrate talents,
  • Motivate others to specialise in an area of interest,
  • Inspire others to also reach higher standards &
  • Raise the standards of Graphic Design in Ghana.
